CERP aims to support local regulation for all the regions in the world. In most cases CERP is very flexible so you can easily add Custom Fields and make Custom Reports to support the regluation of your region.

As of mid-2017, CERP aims to pre-set additional fields and forms required for companies to easily submit reports to the relevant authorities.

This section is still under-development.
